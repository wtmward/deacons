Ping Pong Tournament: Nixon Claims Victory
The Ping Pong tournament delivered intense competition, with Nixon emerging as the champion.

Thrilling Matches

- Lucas and Kobe kicked off the action with a nail-biter, as Lucas edged out Kobe 15-13. They were fated to meetup again in the lower bracket, with Lucas defeating Kobe again 16-14.
- Pierce fought hard in the lower bracket, narrowly beating Dalan 12-10 and then Keyton 12-10.

Finals
In the championship match, Nixon showcased their dominance, defeating Lucas 11-8 to secure the title.

Standouts
Nixon: Undefeated and unstoppable, claiming the crown.
Lucas: Resilient, battling through the lower bracket.
Kallen: Strong performances, reaching the semifinals.
Kobe: Put up a fierce fight in two tight matches against Lucas.
Pierce: Showed grit in the lower bracket with back-to-back close victories.
Keyton: Delivered a solid performance, including an 11-7 win over Tucker.

Congratulations to Nixon and all players for an exciting tournament!
